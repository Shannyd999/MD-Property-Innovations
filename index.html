Who We Are

Founded by David Mitchell and Shannon Daly, MD Property Innovations is driven by a mission to build a meaningful, high-value property portfolio. With backgrounds in finance, logistics, and emergency care, our approach blends efficiency, care, and attention to detail — delivering homes that feel as good as they look.

Our Strategy

We specialise in sourcing below-market-value properties, carrying out high-quality renovations, and either flipping for capital or holding as part of a growing rental portfolio. Our long-term vision includes buy-to-let, BRR deals, and future holiday homes — with every move calculated for sustainable growth.
